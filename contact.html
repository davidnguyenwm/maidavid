Frequently Asked Questions

When do scholarship applications open?

Applications typically open in April each year. Follow us on social media or check our website for announcements.

Who is eligible for the scholarship?

International students currently living in the US under F1 or J1 visas, pursuing undergraduate or graduate studies, with a GPA of 3.0 or higher.

How can I become a mentor?

Please reach out to us via email at maidavid.org@gmail.com expressing your interest in mentorship. We'd love to hear about your background and how you'd like to help.

Is the foundation accepting donations?

Yes! We welcome financial donations to help expand our impact. Please contact us to learn more about donation options.
