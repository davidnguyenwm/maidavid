Our Team

Board of Directors

David T. Nguyen

Board Co-Chair

David is a Research Scientist at Meta. He works with the Stories Team. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from William & Mary.

Mai Anh Do

Board Co-Chair

Mai is a Senior Associate Scientist at Allogene. She works on developing and manufacturing cell therapies for patients with bloodborne and solid cancer. She received her Master's degree in Bioengineering from Santa Clara University.

Advisory Board

Gang Zhou

Professor of Computer Science, William & Mary

Dr. Zhou is a Professor in the Computer Science Department at William & Mary. He was Graduate Program Director from 2015 to 2017. He received his Ph.D. degree from the University of Virginia.

Bill Lu

Bioengineering Associate Professor, Santa Clara University

Dr. Lu is an Associate Professor of Bioengineering at Santa Clara University. He obtained his MD and Ph.D. in Biochemistry and Human Genetics from Shanghai Medical University and University of Manitoba, respectively.

Shabnum Patel

Associate Director, Center for Cancer Cell Therapy, Stanford University

Dr. Patel is an Associate Director of Process Development and Manufacturing for the Mackall Lab at the Center for Cancer Cell Therapy and Laboratory of Cell and Gene Medicine, Stanford University. She earned her Ph.D. in Immunology & Microbiology at George Washington University.
