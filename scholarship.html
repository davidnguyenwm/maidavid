Scholarship Program

The Mai Anh Do & David Nguyen annual scholarship aims to help international students in the US (undergrad and grad) by providing financial support and mentorship throughout university.

We plan to select one scholarship winner each year.

What Scholars Receive

Supporting your academic and professional journey

Financial Stipend

Stipends in the amount of $2,000.00 to support your educational journey.

Personal Mentorship

A dedicated mentor who will provide advice on internship opportunities and professional career development throughout the duration of the scholarship.

Ideal Candidates

Selected international students currently living in the US under F1 or J1 visas should demonstrate academic excellence and personal qualities, including:

  • 3.0+ GPA (on a 4.0 scale)
  • Significant school projects demonstrating initiative and commitment
  • Ability to persevere through tough life challenges or project challenges in your field of study

Application Requirements

Your application should include:

  • Resume — showcasing your academic and professional background
  • Essay — on how you persevered through tough life challenges or project challenges in your field of study with a maximum of 500 words (optional but highly recommended)

Application Timeline

Applications typically open in April each year. Check back or follow us on social media for announcements about future scholarship cycles.
